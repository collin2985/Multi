4. cookedmeat - 40 base durability (NEW: market only)

## CONSUMPTION MECHANICS

### Base System:
- Base consumption rate: 1 durability/minute (total across all food)
- Distribution: Evenly split among ALL food items simultaneously
- Per item rate: consumptionRate / totalFoodItems

### Variety Bonus:
- Formula: Math.max(0.6, 1.0 - (uniqueTypes - 1) × 0.1)
- 1 unique type: 1.0 dur/min (no discount)
- 2 unique types: 0.9 dur/min (10% discount)
- 3 unique types: 0.8 dur/min (20% discount)
- 4 unique types: 0.7 dur/min (30% discount)
- 5+ unique types: 0.6 dur/min (40% discount, capped at 50%)

### Quality Modifier:
- Applied at item creation: durability = baseDurability × (quality / 100)
- Example: Apple at 50% quality = 10 × 0.5 = 5 durability
- Example: Cookedmeat at 100% quality = 40 × 1.0 = 40 durability

### Precision:
- Internal tracking: Full floating-point precision
- Display: Math.floor(item.durability) - whole numbers only
- Clamping: Math.max(0, item.durability) - prevent negatives
- Removal: When durability <= 0, remove item and recalculate distribution

## EXAMPLE SCENARIOS

### Scenario 1: Single Food Type
- 1 apple (10 dur)
- Consumption: 1.0 dur/min
- Duration: 10 minutes

### Scenario 2: Two Food Types
- 1 meat (40 dur) + 2 apples (10 each)
- Variety bonus: 0.9 dur/min
- Per item: 0.9 / 3 = 0.3 dur/min each
- Timeline:
  * 0-33 min: All 3 items consume at 0.3/min
  * 33 min: Apples depleted
  * 33-67 min: Meat continues alone at 0.9/min
  * 67 min: All food gone

### Scenario 3: Many Items
- 10 meat (40 each, all 100% quality)
- Variety bonus: 1.0 dur/min (only 1 type)
- Per item: 1.0 / 10 = 0.1 dur/min each
- Duration: 40 / 0.1 = 400 minutes (6.67 hours)

### Scenario 4: Maximum Variety
- 1 apple + 1 vegetables + 1 cookedfish + 1 cookedmeat
- Variety bonus: 0.7 dur/min (30% discount for 4 types)
- Per item: 0.7 / 4 = 0.175 dur/min each
- Timeline:
  * Apple (10 dur): 10 / 0.175 = 57 minutes
  * Vegetables (20 dur): 20 / 0.175 = 114 minutes
  * Cookedfish (30 dur): 30 / 0.175 = 171 minutes
  * Cookedmeat (40 dur): 40 / 0.175 = 228 minutes
  * But rates change as items deplete!
