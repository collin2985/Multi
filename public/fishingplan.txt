FISHING SYSTEM PLAN
====================

FISHING NET ITEM:
- 2x2 size within backpack inventory
- Texture files: net.png and Rnet.png (to be added)

FISHING MECHANICS:
- Trigger: Player within 0.75 units of terrain with height below 1.02 (water level)
- Action: Shows "Fishing" button in UI
- Animation: Uses existing pickaxe animation (used for most actions)
- Duration: 10 seconds with progress bar (existing action progress bar system)
- Result: Chance to add fish to inventory (if room available)

FISH PROPERTIES:
- Quality: Random value
- Base Durability: 30
- Final Durability: Base * (Quality/100), rounded to whole number
  Example: 50 quality fish = 30 * 0.50 = 15 durability
